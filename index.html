Upload routine
Recommended daily habit for accurate Monday order plans
Every morning, export the latest Parts Ledger and Parts Inventory from your DMS and upload both here. The dashboard, FSN/ABC analysis and order planning all recalculate instantly from whatever is currently loaded — nothing is cached from a previous day. On Monday mornings, after uploading the latest files, go to Order Planning and click Generate this week's plan to get that week's suggested order, automatically capped within your monthly ₹7,00,000 budget.
